# CARE Flagship Example v0.1

## Work

CCRU Writings 1997–2003

## Why this work

CCRU Writings is a strong flagship example because it is large, fragmented, theoretical, occult, philosophical, cybernetic, and difficult to summarise conventionally.

It demonstrates why CARE is needed.

A normal summary would flatten the corpus.

CARE can preserve layered conceptual architecture across many fragments.

## Existing CARE structure

Current known structure:

- Chapter units: 88 CH
- Cluster units: 30 CL
- Metacluster Level 1 units: 8 MC_L1
- Metacluster Level 2 units: 2 MC_L2
- WholeBook unit: 1 WB

## What this proves

This demonstrates that CARE can process a large theoretical corpus into a recursive conceptual hierarchy.

The result is not a summary.

The result is a layered structure that can be inspected at multiple scales.

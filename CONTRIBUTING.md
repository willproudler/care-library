# Contributing to CARE Library

CARE Library v0.1 is an early public documentation and reference release.

The project welcomes corrections, technical criticism, schema discussion, provenance improvements, compatible implementation work, and scholarly responses.

## Public discussion

GitHub issues and pull requests are public. Do not place private CARE Library material, source texts, credentials, personal information, or confidential disclosures in them.

Potential security or accidental-disclosure reports must follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Rights and public boundary

A contribution must not include material that the contributor does not have the right to publish.

Contributions must comply with `CARE_PUBLIC_RELEASE_BOUNDARY_v0.1.md`. In particular, do not submit:

- private source books, RAW texts, passages, annotations, or corpus data;
- private work-level inventories or processing records;
- substantial third-party quotations without an approved release basis;
- unpublished CARE specifications;
- trademark filing evidence or transaction records;
- credentials, private paths, or unnecessary personal information.

Any third-party material must be clearly identified with its source and proposed rights basis.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

All new contributed commits must be signed off under the Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1 contained in the root `DCO` file.

Use:

`git commit --signoff`

A DCO sign-off is a contributor certification, not a copyright assignment. The contributor's sign-off name and email address become part of the public repository history.

Historical commits are not being rewritten solely to add DCO sign-offs.

## Pull requests

Keep pull requests focused and explain:

- what changed;
- why it changed;
- how it was checked;
- whether it includes or derives from third-party material.

Contributors are credited after an accepted contribution, not in anticipation of future work.

The maintainer may request provenance or rights information and may decline material that cannot safely enter the public repository.

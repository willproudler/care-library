## Proposed description

CARE Library v0.1.0 documents a source-grounded, provenance-oriented architecture for transforming long-form texts into layered conceptual structures.

The deposit contains the approved v0.1 architecture, schema, commons and defensive-publication materials.

It does not contain private source books, RAW texts, a public corpus, private databases, generated private corpus outputs, executable CARE software or post-v0.1 specifications.

## DOI workflow

1. Create the Zenodo record as a draft.
2. Enter the approved metadata.
3. Reserve the DOI without publishing the record.
4. Insert the reserved DOI into `CITATION.md`, the validated `CITATION.cff` and the defensive-publication metadata.
5. Freeze the GitHub release commit.
6. Generate the final files, manifest and checksums.
7. Upload only the approved checksum-matched files.
8. Verify the complete Zenodo preview.
9. Obtain explicit publication authorization.
10. Publish the Zenodo record.
11. Verify the DOI and record the public URL.

A reserved DOI must not be described as a published DOI before the Zenodo record is published.

## Exclusions

Do not upload:

- private CARE Library sources or corpus contents;
- RAW texts, private passages or annotations;
- work-level private inventory data;
- generated outputs derived from uncleared sources;
- the withdrawn paper or incomplete flagship example;
- unpublished v0.2 material;
- trademark filing evidence;
- credentials, private paths or personal records;
- permission correspondence or the private rights ledger.

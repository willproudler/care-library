## Source texts and corpus materials

No third-party source-book collection, RAW-text collection, or private CARE corpus is designated as part of the licensed v0.1 repository contents.

References to authors, works, schools, or source lineages do not grant rights in the referenced material.

Any future public source or corpus asset must have an identified release basis recorded in a public rights manifest before publication.

## Other third-party material

Any third-party text, image, dataset, software dependency, or other asset added in the future must be accompanied by:

- its source;
- its copyright or rights status;
- its licence or other release basis;
- any required attribution;
- its inclusion decision.

Material with an unknown, restricted, or unresolved rights basis must not enter a public release.
